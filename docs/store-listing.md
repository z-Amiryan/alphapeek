## Detailed description (paste as-is; keywords woven in naturally)

```
AlphaPeek turns every EVM wallet address and token contract on X (Twitter) into a
hoverable info card. No clicking away, no copy-pasting into a block explorer — just
point at an address and peek.

── WHAT YOU SEE ──

• Hover a wallet address → its total balance, stablecoin allocation, and top
  holdings, read right inside the timeline.

• Hover a token contract → a live quote: symbol, price, 24-hour move, market cap,
  and volume, with a clean 7-day price sparkline.

• Open the toolbar popup → the live Fear & Greed market-mood index, a quick
  address lookup box, and your recent peeks.

── WHY ALPHAPEEK ──

• Fast. The card appears on hover and disappears when you move away. Lookups are
  cached locally so repeat peeks are instant.

• Clean. A single compact card that respects light and dark themes and never
  rewrites the tweet — it only adds a subtle underline on detected addresses.

• Private. No account, no login, no wallet connection, no tracking. AlphaPeek
  never asks you to sign anything and never sees your keys.

── HOW IT WORKS ──

AlphaPeek detects EVM wallet and contract addresses (0x…) in tweets and fetches
read-only market data through a lightweight proxy. It works on x.com and
twitter.com. Crypto data is powered by CoinStats.

── GOOD TO KNOW ──

AlphaPeek is read-only and informational. It does not give financial advice, does
not execute transactions, and is not affiliated with X/Twitter. Always do your own
research.
```

## Search keywords — there is no keyword/tag field

The Web Store has **no** keyword or tag input. Search ranking is driven only by the
**item name**, **short description**, and **detailed description**. Do **not** paste a
keyword list anywhere — a standalone list triggers the "keyword stuffing" rejection
(Yellow Argon — see [troubleshooting](https://developer.chrome.com/docs/webstore/troubleshooting/#keyword-stuffing)).

To stay discoverable, make sure these terms appear **naturally in the prose above**
(they already do): crypto wallet · EVM · token price · contract address · balance ·
holdings · 7-day chart · market cap · Fear & Greed · X / Twitter · on-chain.
